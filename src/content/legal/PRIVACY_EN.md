## 10. Cookies and Comparable Technologies

Our website uses only the following cookies and comparable technologies:

- **Cloudflare security cookies** (e.g., `__cf_bm`): Bot management and protection against automated attacks. Legal basis: § 25 (2) No. 2 TDDDG and Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR.

### 10.2 Functional Cookies / Local Storage (no consent required)

The following values are stored on your end device to ensure the functionality of our website:

Legal basis: § 25 (2) No. 2 TDDDG (technically strictly necessary) and Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in functional website).

Plausible Analytics works cookielessly and without consent. If we use additional analysis or marketing tools in the future that require consent, these will only be activated after your express consent via our cookie banner. This privacy policy will be supplemented accordingly in this case.
